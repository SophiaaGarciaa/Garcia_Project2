Project 2: The Tour
Sophia Garcia | Student ID R#11968034
'The Tour' is a fictional travel magazine that highlights travel destinations, articles, and a gallery of beautiful landscapes. The site features navy and gold editiorial visual deisgn with all 5 pages sharing a consistent navigation, color palette, typography, and information architecture.

Pages Featured
    * Homepage | index.html
        The landing page features a hero with a background image, call to action buttons, and a Top Stories section with a grid of 4 article cards.
    * About Page | about.html
        The about page features a fictional origin story for the site, a detailed bio for three members of the fictional company, and a contact form.
    * Article Page | article.html
        The article page is a full article for "Must See National Parks" created by a fictional character, John Smith. The page includes an article image, a blockquote, and a sidebar with three articles from the landing page.
    * Destination Page | category.html
        The category page is the Destination page which features articles related to travel. The page includes a featured article card navigating directly to the article page and a four column grid of article cards tagged by countries.
    * Gallery Page | gallery.html
        The gallery page features a photo gallery created by a 3 by 2 grid including figure captions. The images were sourced from Unsplash.
