About The Tour

Our Story

Started by just a few writers, The Tour was created to inspire people to take the opportunity to travel. We cover destinations all across the world, from visiting the Eiffel tower to the Egyptian pyramids. The Tour aims to do travel journalism differently by sharing honest, unfiltered, and on the ground reporting. Our small independent publication prides ourself in capturing more than the surface of these beautiful location but also the culture. Our journalists cover hidden gems and budget destination to make your travel planning a simple process.
